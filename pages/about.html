About Cyber Store

What is it?

Cyber Store is a place where you can buy all sorts of cyber products. In the store page you will see a professional pice of art that showcases what the product looks like. All of the most famous cyber products you've heard of can be found here like the Cyber Camel!

Who made it?

Cyber Store was made by Nick Taylor of BYU in class cs260. He did it himself because he forgot to find a partner for the project.

A self portrait made by him.

Why?

Nick Taylor made this store as a way to share with the world all the amazing cyber products that are hard to find. His mission is for everyone to own a cyber product. He also wants to pass the class.
